Capabilities

Everything you need to build
a pipeline from Maps data

Geo Bucket intercepts, normalizes, and enriches Google Maps data in real time. No API keys. No rate limit errors. No server dependencies.

smart_toy

2Captcha Bypass

Automatically solves Google reCAPTCHA challenges during long scraping sessions. No manual intervention required.

hub

Interceptor Pipeline

Patches chrome.storage and JSON.parse at runtime to capture, normalize, and deduplicate lead data before it hits the UI.

travel_explore

Deep Crawl Engine

Extracts hidden emails, social profiles, and phone numbers directly from business websites linked on Google Maps.

shield

Anti-Ban Mechanics

Human-behavior simulation with randomized delays, scroll patterns, and request throttling to protect your Google account.

file_download

One-Click Export

Download your entire lead database as clean CSV or XLSX with column headers, ready for your CRM or cold email tool.

dock_to_right

Side Panel UI

Runs in Chrome's native side panel. Browse Maps normally while Geo Bucket collects data alongside your tab.
